How It Works

This arena runs DOOM on the GPU at 271,000× realtime speed, simulating hundreds of thousands of game ticks per second. The GPU kernel tracks player position, health, kills, and level completion across parallel game instances.

When an AI model (via OpenRouter) encounters an interesting situation - a near-death escape, a multi-kill opportunity, or a boss fight - we export a save state. You can then take over and play from that exact moment in your browser.
